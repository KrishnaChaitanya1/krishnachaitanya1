Books read in 2019

Published: Mon 12 April 2021
By Krishna Chaitanya Reddy

In Books.

I read a lot of books and starting 2019 I made a thing to track what I read. 2019 was mostly dominated by Murakami books don't know for what reason, but once I read "Blind Willow and the Sleeping Woman" I started picking up his books more often.

Here are the books which I read in 2019.

  1. The Count of Monte Cristo by Alexandre Dumas
  2. Blind Willow and the Sleeping Woman by Haruki Murakami
  3. One Hundred Years of Solitude by Gabriel Garcia Marquez
  4. Kafka on the Shore by Haruki Murakami
  5. Sputnik Sweetheart by Haruki Murakami
  6. Men without Women by Haruki Murakami
  7. Third Thoughts by Steve Weinberg
  8. Norwegian Wood by Haruki Murakami
  9. Desire by Haruki Murakami
